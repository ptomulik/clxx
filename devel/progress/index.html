Disclaimer

The clxx project is pretty incomplete. Many things are still missing. This page shall give you precise information on what's already finished and what's not and rough estimation of the overal progress (in percents).

Overview

To estimate the development progress of the clxx project we need to estimate the total amount of work as well. Let's start with the architectural sketch (Fig.1) and split the project up into smaller parts based on its architecture. We should be able then to create lists of things to be done at the lowest levels of decomposition and provide a side-to-side implementation status for these pieces.

The architecture of the clxx software was already explained in The architecture of clxx page. Here we just provide a summary of the progress and links to other documents detailing the development status of the respective pieces.

To see implementation status of certain parts of the clxx projects click on the appropriate region of Fig.1.
